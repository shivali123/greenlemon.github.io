Intro

Long considered the purview of men, financial planning and investment activities are often handled by relatives for the majority of women. This is true despite the fact that during the past ten years, the proportion of women who contribute to home finances has risen. Additionally, women have unique difficulties when it comes to financial planning. These issues must be resolved for them to live a stress-free, financially independent existence.
